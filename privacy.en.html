3. How We Use Information

  • To authenticate users and manage accounts.
  • To provide and manage subscriptions, free trials and free-use limits, and to prevent abuse.
  • To provide notifications/reminders.
  • To investigate and fix bugs and to improve quality and features.
  • To respond to inquiries and provide support.
  • To comply with law and protect the rights, property and safety of us or third parties.

We do not use your personal information beyond the above purposes without your prior consent.

4. Voice Data & AI Processing

Audio you record is sent over an encrypted connection to our AI processing providers (Section 5) and processed for transcription (speech-to-text) and to turn the content into tasks.

  • The resulting transcribed text is stored as part of your tasks in your account.
  • We do not retain the original audio files on an ongoing basis.
  • Our AI processing providers (OpenAI and Anthropic) state in their respective API policies that data submitted via the API is not used to train their AI models. We rely on those policies; please check each provider's site for the latest terms.
  • AI-generated output (breaking down, categorizing and prioritizing tasks) is an assistive process based on your input; you can edit or delete results in the App at any time. You should be aware that you are interacting with an AI system (transparency).
  • We do not create, extract or store voiceprints or any other biometric identifiers from audio. Audio is processed only for transcription and is not used as biometric information to identify you.

Please use your own judgment about whether to include sensitive information (health, beliefs, or other sensitive content) in your voice or text.

No method of transmission or storage is completely secure; we use commercially reasonable measures.

9. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, and data portability regarding your personal information. Contact us at the address in Section 1. You can also edit or delete tasks in the App at any time.

10. Account & Data Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from Settings > "Delete account" in the App. Deletion permanently removes your account, stored tasks, profile and other related data we hold (except data we must retain by law). If in-app deletion is not possible, contact us and we will delete it within a reasonable time after verifying your identity. (To cancel the subscription itself, use Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your device.)

11. Advertising & Tracking

  • The App does not show third-party ads.
  • The App does not track you across apps for advertising and does not request App Tracking Transparency permission.
  • Usage records are used only for providing features, preventing abuse, and improving quality.

12. Subscriptions

Paid features are offered as auto-renewing subscriptions. Purchase, renewal and cancellation are handled through Apple's App Store and billed to your Apple ID. If a free trial applies, it automatically converts to a paid subscription unless cancelled before the trial ends. Payment information is handled by Apple; we do not collect it.

13. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

  • Legal obligation: compliance with applicable law.

You have the rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection and data portability, and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. Data may be transferred to the US and elsewhere with appropriate safeguards. To exercise your rights, contact us at the address in Section 1.
